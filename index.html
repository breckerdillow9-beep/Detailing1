Our Story

Built on pride,
delivered with
precision.

"I started Jackson Auto Glow because I believed every car deserves to be treated with care — and every customer deserves to see real results at their door."

What began as a passion for making vehicles look their best has grown into one of Southeast Missouri's most trusted mobile detailing services. Based in Jackson and proudly serving Cape Girardeau, Fruitland, and surrounding communities — we bring the detail shop to you.

We specialize in high-end transformations: ceramic coatings built to last, paint corrections that reverse years of wear, and interior restorations that make your vehicle feel brand new. Every job gets our full attention. Every result is guaranteed.
