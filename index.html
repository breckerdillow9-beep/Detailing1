Our Story

Built on hard work & local pride

Jackson Auto Glow was started with a simple goal — give people in Jackson and Cape Girardeau a detailing service they can actually count on. No booking a shop weeks out, no dropping your car off all day. We come to you.

Owner Brecker Dillow has put in the work to make sure every vehicle gets treated right. Whether it's a daily driver or something you really care about, we bring the same attention to detail every single time.

We serve Jackson, Cape Girardeau, Fruitland, and the surrounding area — cars, trucks, RVs, boats, you name it.

"I want every customer to feel like their vehicle got the care it deserves. That's what keeps me going."

— Brecker Dillow, Owner
