Built Different

Built on pride,
delivered with precision.

"I started Jackson Auto Glow because I believed every car deserves to be treated with care — and every customer deserves to see real results at their door."

What began as a passion for making vehicles look their absolute best has grown into a trusted mobile detailing business serving Jackson, Cape Girardeau, Fruitland, and communities across Southeast Missouri.

We specialize in high-end transformations — ceramic coatings for long-term protection, paint corrections that remove years of wear, and meticulous interior restorations that make your vehicle feel brand new. Every job gets our full attention, every time.
