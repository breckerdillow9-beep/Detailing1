Driven by Passion

Built on pride,
delivered with precision.

I started Jackson Auto Glow because I believed every car deserves to be treated with care — and every customer deserves to see real results at their door.

What began as a passion for making vehicles look their absolute best has grown into a trusted mobile detailing business serving Jackson, Cape Girardeau, Fruitland, and surrounding communities across Southeast Missouri.

We specialize in high-end transformations — from ceramic coatings that protect your investment long-term, to paint corrections that remove years of wear, to meticulous interior restorations that make your vehicle feel brand new again. Every job gets our full attention, every time.
