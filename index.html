The Story

DRIVEN BYPASSION.

Hey — I'm Brecker Dillow, the founder of Jackson Auto Glow. I started this business because I genuinely care about cars and I believe every vehicle deserves to look its absolute best. What began as a passion in my driveway has grown into a trusted mobile detailing service right here in Jackson and Cape Girardeau, Missouri.

I don't cut corners. Every job gets my full attention — from a deep interior restoration to a full ceramic coating that lasts years. My customers come back because they trust me to deliver showroom-quality results, every single time.

When I show up to your driveway, I'm not just washing your car. I'm protecting your investment. I'm the guy who takes pride in making your vehicle look better than the day you drove it off the lot.
